# A Quick Overview

## Step1: System Compatibility
---
Vpon SDK support:

* iOS：`iOS 8.0 or later & Xcode 9.0 or later`
* Android：`Android 3.0 or later`

Before you start to integrate Vpon SDK, make sure your app is compatible.

## Step2: Register as a Vpon Publisher
---
[Register as a Vpon Publisher][2] first, and you'll get an account to manage your License ID for ad integration and your profit.
